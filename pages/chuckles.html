📖 About the Book

See the world through Chuckles' eyes — and discover that miracles come in all shapes and sizes… even glasses!

Everything around Chuckles looked fuzzy and sometimes a bit scary — until one special trip to the eye doctor changed everything! With the help of his loving family and a cheerful doctor, Chuckles learns that sometimes the clearest vision comes from faith, love, and a brand-new pair of glasses.

This beautifully illustrated picture book invites children into a heartwarming adventure of discovery, friendship, and faith. Through gentle humor and tender moments, Chuckles reminds every child that they are wonderfully made, deeply loved, and perfectly unique — just the way God intended.

Whether your child is getting their first pair of glasses, needs a boost of self-confidence, or simply loves a joyful story about a character they can cheer for, Chuckles is the perfect companion.
