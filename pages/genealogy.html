Featured Story: Grandaddy Herring's Journey to the Holy Land

In 1969, Robert "Bob" Herring — "Grandaddy Herring" — traveled to Israel on the journey of a lifetime. He kept a journal of everything he saw and experienced. Tragically, he suffered a heart attack at the Tel Aviv airport while boarding his flight home, and passed away there in Israel. His journal remains a cherished piece of family history.

How the Lines Connect

Charles Larry Crews Jr. carries the Crews paternal line (Georgia → Alabama → Florida, since 1736). His wife, Alycia Stringer Crews, carries the Stringer line (Norman England → Cheshire → Virginia → Alabama, since the 1590s) as well as the Herring and Boutwell lines through her family's Alabama roots. When Charles and Alycia married, these three great family lines — Crews, Stringer, and Herring — came together into one.
