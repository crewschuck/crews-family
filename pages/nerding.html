    Rogue One (2016)
    Anthology · Director: Gareth Edwards
    The dark horse that exceeded everyone's expectations. The Vader hallway scene is the greatest minute in Star Wars history. No notes.
    ★★★★★
  • 2
    The Empire Strikes Back (1980)
    Episode V · Director: Irvin Kershner
    Universally agreed upon. The greatest sequel ever made. "I am your father" still gives everyone chills even knowing it's coming.
    ★★★★★
  • 3
    A New Hope (1977)
    Episode IV · Director: George Lucas
    Where it all started. James saw this in theaters in 1977 and brought Michael to see the re-release in 1997. The original is sacred.
    ★★★★★
  • 4
    Return of the Jedi (1983)
    Episode VI · Director: Richard Marquand
    Controversial placement — some say top 3. The Ewoks remain a point of contention at every reunion. James defends the Ewoks and will not be moved.

    Revenge of the Sith (2005)
    Episode III · Director: George Lucas
    "Hello there." The only prequel that earns its spot. The opera scene is genuinely great cinema, and the tragedy of Anakin lands when you've watched everything first.
    ★★★★

Where it all started. James watched this in first-run as a kid and passed it down. Kirk, Spock, and McCoy remain the defining Trek trio. Dated but endlessly charming.
