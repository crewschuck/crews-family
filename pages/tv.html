🚀 Sci-Fi Favorites

🌌Syfy/Amazon
The Expanse
Sci-Fi / Thriller · 2015–2022 · 6 Seasons
★★★★★
The most scientifically accurate science fiction series ever put on screen. A solar system colonized, fractured, and on the brink of war — with a detective story at its heart. Holden, Naomi, Amos, and Avasarala are all-timers. Criminally underrated when it aired; a genuine masterpiece.
✅ CompletedEssential
🌠Apple TV+
Foundation
Sci-Fi / Epic · 2021– · 3 Seasons
★★★★½
Isaac Asimov's sweeping, centuries-spanning story of the fall of a galactic empire and one mathematician's plan to shorten the dark age that follows. Gorgeous visuals and genuinely epic scope. Takes patience — and repays it.
✅ Caught upEpic
🚀Fox (2002)
Firefly
Sci-Fi / Western · 2002–2003 · 1 Season
★★★★★
Joss Whedon's masterwork — a space western about a crew of misfits on the fringes of civilization. Cancelled after one season. Fourteen episodes and a film, and it left a hole in sci-fi culture that nothing has ever filled. Serenity is required viewing after the series.
💬 "You can't take the sky from me."
✅ Completed (criminally)Gone Too Soon
👽Fox
The X-Files
Sci-Fi / Thriller · 1993–2018 · 11 Seasons
★★★★★
Mulder and Scully — the original skeptic vs. believer dynamic. Monster-of-the-week episodes, a vast government conspiracy mythology, and two of the most compelling characters in TV history. Seasons 1–7 are required viewing. The truth is out there.
✅ Classic SeasonsIconic
🔭Fox
Fringe
Sci-Fi / Thriller · 2008–2013 · 5 Seasons
★★★★★
FBI agent Olivia Dunham, a mad scientist, and his son investigate fringe science — parallel universes, genetic experiments, and Pattern events tied to a vast corporate conspiracy. Walter Bishop is one of the greatest characters ever written. The parallel universe storyline in Season 3 is some of the best TV in the 2000s.
✅ CompletedUnderrated
🚀Sci Fi Channel
Battlestar Galactica (2004)
Sci-Fi / Drama · 2004–2009 · 4 Seasons
★★★★★
The reimagining that proved science fiction could be prestige drama. Cylons have a plan. Humanity survives by a thread. A political, philosophical war epic disguised as a space opera — and one of the greatest shows of its era. Start with the 2003 miniseries.
✅ CompletedPrestige
🌀Sci Fi Channel
Farscape
Sci-Fi / Adventure · 1999–2003 · 4 Seasons
★★★★½
An astronaut is accidentally shot through a wormhole to the far side of the galaxy and ends up on a living ship with a crew of escaped alien prisoners. Wildly creative, often bonkers, emotionally devastating in the final season. John Crichton is one of sci-fi's great protagonists.
✅ CompletedHidden Gem
🌌PTEN / TNT
Babylon 5
Sci-Fi / Epic · 1993–1998 · 5 Seasons
★★★★★
The first truly serialized epic in science fiction television — planned from beginning to end with a complete arc. A space station at the center of an interstellar war with gods, prophecy, and politics. Seasons 2–4 are among the greatest TV ever produced. J. Michael Straczynski wrote 92 of 110 episodes himself.
✅ CompletedEssential Sci-Fi

The Office
Mockumentary · 2005–2013 · 9 Seasons
★★★★★
Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Scranton, Pennsylvania. The evolution from cringe comedy to genuine warmth over nine seasons is one of TV's great arcs. Dwight Schrute is a comedy icon. The finale makes people cry every single time.

Leslie Knope and the Parks Department of Pawnee, Indiana. A show that started as an Office knockoff and became something entirely its own — genuinely optimistic, warm, and one of the most rewatchable comedies ever made. Ron Swanson is a god.
